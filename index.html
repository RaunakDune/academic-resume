About

I am a Geospatial Data Scientist with a focus on ML/AI, with a particular specialization in remote sensing, geotechnical engineering, geostatistics, agent-based modeling and visual place recognition. I have worked in blockchain-based edge computing, post-disaster recovery systems and Lunar exploration methodologies. I enjoy lecturing and teaching undergraduate students.
